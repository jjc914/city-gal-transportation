is the owner and primary operator of City Gal Transportation. Ginny is a proud member of the Northfield Chamber of Commerce.

From Northfield, for Northfield.

City Gal Transportation started in 2021 when Northfield resident Ginny Klinger noticed a need in the community for a reliable and local car service. Since then, City Gal Transportation has grown to include three drivers to meet demand.

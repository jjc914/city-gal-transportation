Taxi

Book a comfortable and reliable ride to wherever you need to go. Travel within Northfield is $10, call 612-840-1687 for out-of-town rates.

Airport

Seamless airport transfers take the stress out of travel. Trust us to get you to and from the terminal ready for the day. Save money on rideshares: $75 for 1, $80 for 2, $90 for 3, and $100 for 4.

Medical

Caring medical transportation services for safe journeys to and from your medical appointments. There is a $20 surcharge between 10pm and 6am and a $25 additional charge for Holidays.

From Northfield, for Northfield.

City Gal Transportation started in 2021 when Northfield resident Ginny Klinger noticed a need in the community for a reliable and local car service. Since then, City Gal Transportation has grown to include three drivers to meet demand. We accept cash, Venmo, or credit cards with Square.
